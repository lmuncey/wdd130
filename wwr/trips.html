The most exciting trip you'll ever take on water!

1 Hour Trip

This trip is made especially for beginners. Spend one hour out on the water and experience some mild rapids. Floating and/or swimming alongside the raft is typically permitted because the water in this section is usually calm.

2 Hour Trip

This trip is for people with a little more experience on the river. Starting a little ways downstream, this trip encounters some larger rapids, deeper water, and faster currents. For people seeking a moderate level of thrill!

4 Hour Trip

This trip is for rafters who know what they are doing! The rapids on this part of the river can get up to Class VI & Class V, depending on the season. The water is fast, and the excitement is at an all-time high!
